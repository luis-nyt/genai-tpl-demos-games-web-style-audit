# LinkBlock
`LinkBlock` wraps a [`Flex` component](https://foundation.nyt.net/?path=/docs/tokens-layout-flex--basic#flex).
It is used to extend the clickable area of a hyperlink to a group of elements by using a nested [`Link` component](/docs/link--default-story).

Use `LinkBlock.Link` to extend a single hyperlink to encompass the entire surface of `LinkBlock` and its child elements.
Within `LinkBlock`, you can use a plain `Link` to for another secondary hyperlink, e.g., a byline.

This component is the equivalent to a "Card" component in many design systems, but is modified here to structure content only.
Both are used to create a hyperlink out of content, typically text and images, about a single topic such as an article.
